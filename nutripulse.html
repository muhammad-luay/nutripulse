Why NutriPulse?

Uganda faces a double burden of malnutrition — ~29% stunting among children, ~28% anemia among women, and widespread micronutrient gaps. Fragmented data and limited analytics slow effective action. NutriPulse unifies real‑time nutrition data, predictive modeling, economic planning, and supply chain optimization into a single decision‑support hub.

Problem: Fragmented Evidence & Inefficient Spend

  • Disparate datasets across programs and partners.
  • Limited ability to simulate coverage, costs, and health outcomes before investing.
  • Intervention design often ignores nutrient synergies and supply constraints.

Solution: A National Command Center

  • Data Intelligence: ingest, validate, and harmonize national nutrition data (12 validated sources, 9,812 records; ±2.5% margin).
  • Predictive Models: risk scoring, coverage estimation, and nutrient gap predictions.
  • Economic Planning: ROI, NPV, BCR with scenario analysis and budget optimization.
